The Brainstorming Process

  1. Brainstorm Problems
  2. Vector Ideation* (Specify problem areas)
  3. Pick a Problem
  4. Speak to potential customers
  5. Brainstorm solutions
  6. Pick a solution

*Vector Ideation is the process of brainstorming issues specific to different "vectors" or categories of an individual's life. (Ex: Transition from High School to College, Relationships, Personal Health, etc.)
