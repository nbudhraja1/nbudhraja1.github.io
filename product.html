The Product

The Problem

  • Teenagers, in particular girls, are following fad diets and workout plans in the pursuit of remaining healthy
  • Workout websites and apps are weight-loss oriented, which should not be a priority for growing teenagers
  • Motivation for working out for many young girls = appearance-oriented
  • People are not eating the food that will help maximize their workouts
  • Young women feel intimidated to use weights at the gym

The Solution

With the increase of phone usage amongst young people, an mobile application is the best way to establish truly healthy habits amongst young women. This mobile application provides workout guides for specific areas of muscle growth/physical health with instructional videos, a mandatory food suggestion page (with optional alerts throughout the day) that provides users with the knowledge of which foods should be eaten pre and post workout, and a calendar for the user to truly commit to fitness by scheduling time to workout.
